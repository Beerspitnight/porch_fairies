About Us

Porch Fairies are a seasonal porch-decorating service based in Monmouth & Ocean County, NJ.

We specialize in curated, high-quality fall and Halloween displays designed to bring beauty, charm, and magic to your doorstep—without you lifting a finger.

From pumpkins and hay bales to specialty gourds and corn stalks, each porch is styled with care, creativity, and a touch of fairy dust. 

We offer multiple package tiers, full setup, and optional takedown, so your home can feel festive and polished all season long.

Whether you’re looking to wow the neighbors, prep for photos, or simply enjoy a beautifully styled entryway—we’ve got you covered.
